Busy Bishop is a crowdsourced typographical correction tool.

How it works

You see a typo on a web page, a news article or blog post or really any web page. You suggest an edit with the browser extension. The owner or publisher of the page uses the extenstion to see the edits the community has suggested.

Select a small amount of text containing the mistake you'd like to correct. Its simple. Just select Its in the preceeding sentence. Right click on the selection and choose Suggest Edit. In the dialog, type It's and click OK.

You can click the extension's browser action button to see suggestions made by others.

For page owners

After installing the browser extension, click the browser action button to see a list of the suggestions. Make any changes you choose to your documents and publish them the normal way. Simple.

What's with the name? What does it mean?

The name was generated by a tool as a temporary project name. It just kind of stuck. It has no other meaning.
